What is the Fibonacci Sequence?

The Fibonacci Sequence is a mathmatical rythem that is often found in nature through things like sunflower seeds.

It was discovered by Leonardo Fibonacci in 1202. Its simple, heres how: 0 + 1 = 1, 1 + 1 = 2, 2 + 1 = 3, 3 + 1 = 4 ...
